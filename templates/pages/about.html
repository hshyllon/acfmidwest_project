Discover About ACF HISTORY

learn about our HISTORY

Founded in 1977 as an outreach ministry to African Students, ACF-USA has since grown to over fifty (50) chapters located in several cities across the United States of America. In 1983, the Midwest Region comprised of Minnesota, Minnesota, Ohio, Iowa, Michigan, Illinois, Wisconsin, Indiana, N. Dakota, S. Dakota, Nebraska, and Kansas was established, with the addition of the Columbus chapter in 1989. The region held its first conference that year during the Easter weekend at Mishawka, Indiana. Subsequent conferences have been Wisconsin '84 (Twin Cities Chapter), Chicago '85, Wisconsin '86, Michigan '87, Michigan '88, Chicago '89, Ohio '90, Wisconsin '91 (Twin Cities Chapter), and more.

Discover About ACF VALUES

learn about our values statement

We believe in:

  • God the creator of all things
  • Jesus Christ as God manifested in the flesh
  • The Holy Spirit, whose purpose is to empower us and lead us into righteousness
  • The Holy Bible as the Word of God
  • Love, Sharing, and Fellowship of all Christians

Who can be an ACF member?

Membership is open to all African Christians, as well as to all Christians who have been called to minister to Africa and Africans.
